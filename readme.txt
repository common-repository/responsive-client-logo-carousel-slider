Logo Carousel is an easy-to-use and versatile WordPress plugin that enables you to showcase a responsive carousel slider of your clients' logos or images on your website. With Logo Carousel, you can create an engaging and professional display of your clients' logos to help build trust with your visitors.

The plugin comes with a range of customizable settings, allowing you to create a carousel slider that seamlessly matches your site's branding and design. You can customize the slider's animation style, speed, and the number of images to display. Additionally, you can add links to each image, redirecting your visitors to your clients' websites or pages.

Logo Carousel is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring that your carousel slider looks great and functions smoothly across all devices and screen sizes. You can further customize the slider's appearance using CSS or the built-in style editor, giving you the flexibility to create a unique and customized slider that matches your website's look and feel.

The plugin is user-friendly and easy to install, with a simple interface that allows you to add and manage your clients' logos or images with ease. You can upload images directly from your media library or import them from an external URL, and the plugin will automatically generate a carousel slider for you.

Logo Carousel is highly flexible and adaptable to your needs. You can use it to showcase your clients' logos, display customer testimonials, or even to showcase your own work or portfolio. The possibilities are endless!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload `plugin-directory` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use shortcode in page, post or in widgets.
4. If you want player in your theme php, Place `<?php echo do_shortcode('YOUR_SHORTCODE'); ?>` in your templates
